Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

WebClip collects minimal data:

  • License key and activation status: Stored locally in your browser's sync storage to verify your Pro license.
  • No browsing history: WebClip does not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history or the content of pages you visit.

2. How We Use Information

  • License data is used solely to verify your Pro subscription status.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to process payments (via Paddle).

3. Payment Processing

Payments are handled by Paddle (paddle.com), our authorized reseller and Merchant of Record. Paddle's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.

4. Data Storage

All extension data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. No data is sent to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

WebClip may optionally sync notes to third-party services (Notion, Obsidian, etc.) that you configure. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services.

6. Children's Privacy

WebClip is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify users of significant changes via the extension update notes.
