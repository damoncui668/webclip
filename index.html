Frequently asked questions

How do I export a ChatGPT conversation to Notion or Obsidian?
Open the chat, click the WebClip icon, and the conversation is converted to clean Markdown in the side panel. Pick Notion, Obsidian, Yuque or WizNote, and it syncs in one click — images included.
Can I export Claude, Gemini or DeepSeek chats too?
Yes. WebClip works across major AI platforms — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek and more — plus 30+ regular sites like docs and tech communities.
Does it keep code blocks, tables and images?
Yes. WebClip converts to clean Markdown that preserves headings, lists, tables, code blocks and images — so a clipped article or AI answer lands properly formatted, not as a wall of plain text you have to fix by hand.
Does WebClip support Yuque (语雀) or WizNote (为知)?
Yes — natively. WebClip is one of the only clippers that syncs to Yuque and WizNote directly, alongside Notion, Obsidian, Typora and local files.
Can I edit the clip before it's saved?
Yes. There's a real-time Markdown editor with live preview in the side panel. Trim, annotate and tidy the content before it lands in your vault.
Is my data uploaded anywhere?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser. WebClip has no servers and collects no browsing history. Content goes only to the notes account you explicitly connect.
Does it work on regular articles, not just AI chats?
Absolutely. The same one-click flow turns any article, doc or web page into clean Markdown, stripping ads and noise — across 30+ supported sites.
Is WebClip free?
Core clipping, reading and Markdown export are free forever. Pro (PDF/Word/long-image export and cloud sync) is a low-cost annual or one-time lifetime plan.
